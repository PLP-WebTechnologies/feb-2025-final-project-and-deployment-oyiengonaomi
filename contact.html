Frequently Asked Questions

Can I submit my own recipe to Global Flavors?

+

Yes! We welcome recipe submissions from our community. Please use our recipe submission form or email your recipe with high-quality photos to recipes@globalflavors.com. Our culinary team will review and may feature it on our site with full credit to you.

How can I suggest a cuisine or food culture to be featured?

+

We're always looking to expand our coverage of global cuisines. If you'd like to suggest a cuisine or food culture, please email us at content@globalflavors.com with your suggestion and any specific dishes you'd like to see featured.

Do you offer cooking classes or workshops?

+

Yes, we host virtual cooking classes every month and in-person workshops in select cities quarterly. Check our Events page for upcoming classes or subscribe to our newsletter to be notified about new events.

I can't find a specific ingredient mentioned in your recipes. What should I do?

+

Many of our recipes include ingredient substitutions in the notes section. If you can't find a recommendation there, please contact us and our culinary team will suggest suitable alternatives that maintain the authentic flavor profile of the dish.

Do you have dietary-specific recipes (vegetarian, gluten-free, etc.)?

+

Absolutely! You can filter our recipe collection by dietary preferences using the tags on our Recipes page. We're constantly adding new recipes to accommodate various dietary needs while preserving authentic global flavors.
